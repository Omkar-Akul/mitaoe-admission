A Legacy of Excellence in Technical Education

Established in 1999, MIT Academy of Engineering (MITAOE) is one of Maharashtra's premier engineering institutions, approved by AICTE and affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University.

Our vision is to produce globally competent engineers who are ethical, innovative, and ready to solve real-world problems through technology.

Your Journey Starts Here

Simple, transparent admission process. Our AI counsellor is available 24/7 to guide you.

1

Check Eligibility

10+2 with PCM. Min. 45% aggregate (40% reserved).

2

Appear in MHT-CET / JEE

Valid MHT-CET or JEE Main score required.

3

Fill Application

Register and fill CAP application on the DTE portal.

4

Document Verification

Submit mark sheets, caste certificate, domicile & photo ID.

5

Allotment & Admission

Accept seat allotment and report to college.
